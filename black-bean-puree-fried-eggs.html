Black Bean Purée & Fried Eggs with Avocado & Grape Tomatoes

30 minutes | 1 serving

Grab ingredients

  • 75 g avocado
  • 150 g cooked black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 80 ml low-sodium chicken or vegetable broth
  • 3 large eggs
  • 75 g grape tomatoes
  • 20 g jalapeño pepper, optional
  • 60 g yellow onion
  • 10 g ground flaxseed
  • 1 tsp extra virgin olive oil, for bean purée
  • 1 tsp extra virgin olive oil, for eggs
  • ¼ tsp chili powder
  • ¼ tsp ground cumin
  • ⅛ tsp salt
  • ⅛ tsp black pepper

Cook & enjoy

  1. Wash and dry the fresh produce: grape tomatoes, avocado, and jalapeño.
  2. Preheat a small saucepan over medium heat. Finely dice 60 g yellow onion.
  3. Add 1 tsp olive oil to the saucepan. Cook the onion until softened, about 3-4 minutes. Add the diced jalapeño and cook for another 30 seconds.
  4. Drain and rinse 150 g cooked black beans. Add to the saucepan with 80 ml broth, ¼ tsp chili powder, ¼ tsp cumin, ⅛ tsp salt, and ⅛ tsp black pepper. Bring to a gentle simmer.
  5. Mash the beans using a potato masher or immersion blender until mostly smooth. Stir in 10 g ground flaxseed and cook another minute until slightly thickened. Remove from heat and cover.
  6. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add 1 tsp olive oil and cook 3 eggs until the whites are set and the yolks remain runny, about 2-3 minutes.
  7. Halve 75 g grape tomatoes and dice 75 g avocado. Mix together in a bowl. Season lightly with a pinch of salt and pepper if desired.
  8. To serve, spoon the black bean purée into a bowl, top with the fried eggs, and serve the avocado and tomato salad on the side. Enjoy.
